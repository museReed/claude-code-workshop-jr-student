# Literature Comparison Table

| 論文 | 研究問題 | 方法 | 樣本規模 | 主要發現 | 局限 |
|------|----------|------|----------|----------|------|
| Chen, Mitchell & Okafor (2024) — AI Adoption in UK SMEs | What factors most strongly predict AI tool adoption in UK SMEs in the post-LLM era? | Sequential explanatory mixed methods: survey (logistic regression) + semi-structured interviews (thematic analysis) | n=287 survey + 18 interviews | Competitive pressure is the strongest predictor of AI adoption (β=0.42, p<0.001); cost-as-barrier narrative is outdated — only 34% cited cost as primary obstacle. | Cross-sectional design cannot establish causality; sample over-represents London/South East (61%); data collected pre-GPT-4. |
| Almeida & Yamamoto (2023) — Platform Strategy and Generative AI | How does generative AI integration alter strategic positioning and value capture of two-sided platforms? | Longitudinal multi-case study: 3 platforms across distinct verticals | 47 interviews + 280+ internal documents across 3 cases | Generative AI shifts platforms from "matchmakers" to "co-producers" — they now generate content rather than just routing it, creating new liability exposure. | Small case sample (n=3) limits generalisability; UK/EU only; study predates EU AI Act enforcement. |
| Hoffmann, Singh & Carvalho (2024) — Skills Mismatch in Digital Transformation | Which managerial capabilities most strongly differentiate successful from unsuccessful digital transformation outcomes? | Cross-sectional survey + follow-up interviews; structural equation modelling + framework analysis | n=412 managers across 4 sectors + 26 interviews | Technical skill depth has weak correlation with transformation success (r=0.18), while judgement skills (scope, evaluate, override) have strong correlation (r=0.61). | Self-report bias; "success" is self-rated without objective metrics; excludes manufacturing and public sector. |

## 最支撐核心論點的論文

**Hoffmann et al. (2024)** 最直接支撐論點。原因：

1. 論點主張「judgement-augmented workflows」是 AI 採用成功的分水嶺 — Hoffmann 直接量化了 judgement skills 與轉型成功的相關性 (r=0.61 vs technical skills r=0.18)
2. 論文提出的「judgement-augmented automation」框架正是 outline.md Chapter 3 要延伸的理論基礎
3. 數據顯示企業在技術訓練的投資是判斷力訓練的 8 倍 — 支撐「tool adoption ≠ capability building」的核心論點

Chen et al. (2024) 則提供 UK SME 的採用現狀（回答「是否在採用」），Almeida & Yamamoto (2023) 提供平台層面的宏觀視角，但都沒有直接觸碰「從工具使用到能力建構」這個核心問題。
