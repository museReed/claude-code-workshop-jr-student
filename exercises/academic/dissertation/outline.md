## Research Question

How do UK small and medium enterprises (SMEs) move from ad-hoc generative AI tool use to building durable organisational capabilities, and what determines whether this transition succeeds?

## Argument (rough)

Most SMEs treat generative AI as a productivity tool, but the firms that gain lasting advantage are the ones that build **judgement-augmented workflows** — combining AI output with human evaluation gates. Tool adoption is necessary but insufficient; capability is the real differentiator.
